# Business-Nexus

NEXUS · Global Elite Network

Business school will teach you frameworks. Case studies. Models.
But it will never show you the map.
The real one — of who owns what, who supplies whom, who's quietly winning.
That map doesn't exist in any textbook. So I built it.


What is NEXUS?
NEXUS is an interactive global map of the world's most influential founders, CEOs, and business leaders — visualized as a live network of relationships.
It was born in a graph theory class at IMT. Halfway through a lecture on nodes, edges, and network structures, one thought became impossible to ignore:

The entire global business world is a graph. And nobody has drawn it yet.

NEXUS is that graph.

Why it matters
Most people try to understand industries by reading reports and following the news. But the real logic of any industry lives somewhere else — in the people who built it, and in the relationships between them.
Before you study what they built — understand who they are. That's where it all begins.
NEXUS makes this visible:

Open any country → instantly see its business ecosystem: which industries dominate, who leads them, how they connect
Focus on any industry → filter to tech, finance, luxury, energy, and more — and watch the network reorganize around that lens
Click any person → a full profile slides in: role, net worth, biography, key tags, relationship network, and direct links to Forbes, Wikipedia & LinkedIn
Follow the edges → supply chains (→), rivalries (⚡), and partnerships (◇) are all drawn out — visible, labelled, and explorable

How to Use

Explore the network — nodes are colored by industry sector
Filter by sector — use the top tag bar to isolate an industry
Click any person node — a profile card slides in from the right
Follow the links — Forbes, Wikipedia, and LinkedIn are one click away
Switch views — toggle between Graph (⬡) and Card (⊞) mode anytime

这是什么？
NEXUS（纽链） 是一张可交互的全球商业精英图谱，将全球最具影响力的创始人、CEO 和商业领袖，以真实的关系网络形式可视化呈现。
它诞生于我在 IMT 的一堂图论课。教授讲到节点、边和网络结构——讲到一半，我突然意识到：

全球商业世界本身就是一张图。只是从来没有人把它画出来。

NEXUS 就是这张图。

为什么它重要
大多数人试图通过读报告、刷新闻来理解一个行业。但任何行业真正的逻辑，藏在别的地方——藏在建造它的人身上，藏在他们彼此之间的关系里。
在研究他们做了什么之前，先搞清楚他们是谁。这才是一切的起点。
NEXUS 让这件事变得可见：

点开任何国家 → 立刻看清这个国家的商业生态：哪些产业是支柱、谁在主导、彼此如何关联
聚焦任何行业 → 按科技、金融、奢侈品、能源等筛选，图谱即时重建
点击任意人物 → 右侧滑出完整名片：职位、资产、简介、标签、关系网络，以及 Forbes / Wikipedia / LinkedIn 直达链接
顺着连线走 → 供应关系（→）、竞争关系（⚡）、合作关系（◇）全部可见，有标注，可探索
